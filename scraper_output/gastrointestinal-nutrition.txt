Vision: To strengthen the role of nutrition as a core part of GI care across all healthcare settings. Mission: Create clear nutrition guidelines for common GI conditions. Train and support healthcare teams in GI nutrition. Work together with doctors, dietitians, and nurses for better patient care. Encourage simple, practical, patient-friendly nutrition solutions.
