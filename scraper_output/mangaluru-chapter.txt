On the occasion of International Women’s Day, IAPEN India – Mangaluru Chapter organized a webinar in collaboration with Alva’s College (Autonomous), Moodbidri, to promote awareness on women’s health and nutrition. World Dietetics Day 2026- Webinar Date : 12th January 2026 Platform : Google meet Beneficiaries: 25 students and 5 faculty members Webinar was hosted by the chapter to commemorate World Dietetics Day on the topic “Role of nutrition in cancer care: From diagnosis to survivorship “ by Ms. Daveena Pinto, Clinical Nutritionist & IAPEN member ,Mangalore Institute of Oncology, Mangalore. The main objective of the session is to enhance the clinical knowledge of students and faculty regarding the critical impact of nutritional intervention throughout the cancer journey. The webinar focused on the nutritional assessment of the patient ,management of side-effects through diet to ensure complete recovery and also on long term dietary strategies to improve quality of life, regain strength and potentially reduce the risk of recurrence. Total of 25 students and 5 faculties benefitted from the program. The program successfully highlighted the indispensable role of dietitians in the multidisciplinary approach to cancer treatment. The Women Conclave – Nurturer the Nurturer was held at VS Acharya Hall, Alvas College , bringing together over 800 beneficiaries from diverse backgrounds. The conclave served as a powerful platform dedicated to celebrating, educating, and empowering women at every stage of life. The event hosted eminent panellists from across the country , representing various fields. Each expert shared unique insights and real-life experiences, creating a rich and interconnected dialogue. The discussions provided an integrated, holistic approach to enhancing women’s health —from puberty through reproductive years and into menopause.
