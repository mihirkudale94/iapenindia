Generate nationally representative, practical evidence on diet–brain links across life-course and neuro conditions. 2. Develop and publish India-adapted clinical practice guidelines and protocols for neuro-nutrition. 3. Train and certify multidisciplinary professionals (neurologists, dietitians, rehab teams, nurses) in standard neuro-nutrition competencies. 4. Support development and validation of affordable Indian dietary therapies and enteral/ketogenic formulations. 5. Advocate with national agencies (NIN, ICMR, state health) to include neuro-nutrition in policy, public health programs, and medical curricula. Vision: An India where nutrition is a recognized, evidence-based pillar of prevention, treatment and rehabilitation for brain and mental health across every community and care level. Mission: 1.To produce India-relevant evidence, guidelines and training within 3 years. 2.To catalyse 3 multicentre research projects (paediatric neurodevelopment, dementia risk, epilepsy dietary therapy in 24 months. 3. To Launch a certified 6-month Neuro-Nutrition Practitioner course tailored for India (online + hands-on) in year 1. 4. To publish an India-adapted “Neuro-Nutrition Toolkit” (clinical algorithms, sample meal plans, enteral recipes, monitoring checklists) within 12 months. 5.To secure formal liaison with one national agency (NIN/ICMR/Ministry of Health) to pilot policy integration and one state pilot program in year Meet The Team: Founder Mrs Vibha Hasija Qualification : MSc (Foods, Nutrition and Dietetics); P.G.
